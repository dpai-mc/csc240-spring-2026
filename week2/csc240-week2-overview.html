Learning Objectives

By the end of this week, you will be able to:

  • Identify properties of relations (reflexive, symmetric, transitive)
  • Determine if a relation is an equivalence relation
  • Classify functions as injective, surjective, or bijective
  • Compose functions and find inverse functions
  • Apply the pigeonhole principle to solve problems
