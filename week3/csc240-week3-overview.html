üí° Study Strategies

  • Master truth tables: Practice until you can construct them quickly and accurately. They're fundamental for everything that follows.
  • Understand cardinality intuitively: The concept of "same size" for infinite sets is counterintuitive. Work through examples until it makes sense.
  • Connect to programming: Every time you write an if-statement or Boolean expression, you're using the logic from this week.
  • Start Quiz 1 prep early: This quiz covers three weeks of material. Don't wait until the last minute to review.
  • Time management: This is a heavy week with Quiz 1 preparation. Spread your work throughout the week.
